Privacy Policy – EmotionLift

EmotionLift (“the App”) is a mobile application designed to help users track their moods and emotions. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

EmotionLift may store information that you enter into the app, such as:

  • Mood selections
  • Emotion choices
  • Optional notes you add for your own reflection

This information is stored **on your device**. We do not personally identify you through this data.

2. Data Storage

Your mood entries and notes are stored **locally on your device**. If you uninstall the app, this data may be deleted from your device.

If the app uses third-party services (such as analytics or advertising networks), those services may collect certain information as described in their own privacy policies.

3. Third-Party Services

EmotionLift may use third-party services such as:

  • Google Firebase / Analytics (for anonymous usage statistics)
  • Google AdMob or other ad networks (if ads are shown)

These services may collect information such as device identifiers, app usage, and general location data. This is used to improve app performance and show relevant ads.

Please refer to the privacy policies of these providers for more details.

4. Children’s Privacy

EmotionLift is not directed to children under 13. If you are under 13, please use this app only with the permission of a parent or guardian and according to local laws.

5. Not Medical Advice

EmotionLift is not a medical, psychological, or crisis service. It does not provide professional diagnosis or treatment. If you are experiencing a mental health emergency, please contact local emergency services or a qualified professional.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
